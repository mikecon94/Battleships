How to play

Game Objectives

Sink all 5 of your opponents ships, before they sink yours!

Before the Game Begins
  1. At the start of the game every player chooses a selection of five ships.

  2. Both players can choose the map they want and then a random number generator decides which player gets their chosen map.

  3. Both players place their five ships on their respective playing fields, ships can be oriented either vertically or horizontally.

  4. Players choose one power up to employ in the game.

  5. Once all ships are placed, press anywhere to play.
